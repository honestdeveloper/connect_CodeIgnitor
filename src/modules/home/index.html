What is 6Connect?

It is the easiest way for enterprises to deliver your products and documents safely to your customers.

  • Review and choose the courier services you need
  • Deliver your goods / items to your customers
  • Rate the deliveries. Change couriers with bad services.
An enterprise grade single-point delivery management system for enterprises to pass delivery orders to the couriers / freight operators in a hassle-free manner.

How?

Via 6Connect, your employees / colleagues can place delivery orders to different couriers and monitor them from the same dashboard.
1.

Your employees will login to 6Connect and place delivery orders to your pre-approved services,

2.

Selected couriers will receive the order via email. They will come to collect and deliver the parcels.

3.

Courier will update the delivery status and your employees will be able to monitor the progress from 6Connect and get notfied.

Benefits

  • Freedom to choose

    Gain instant access to a networks of local couriers and freight service providers.

  • Single Dashboard

    With 6Connect, your employees can manage deliveries from all our courier from a single dashboard.

  • Accountability

    Track the usage of every departments and employees. Prevent misuse of company resources for personal use.

  • Get best services

    Our benchmark technology measures the performance of our couriers to ensure you get the best rates and quality.

  • Remember you

    6Connect helps to remember your entry and allows you to do similar deliveries without keying in details from the scratch.

  • Switch anytime

    Not happy with a courier? No worries, switch to another courier without even your employees realised it.
